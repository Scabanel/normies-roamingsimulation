# Normies World Simulation

A living 3D globe where every Normie NFT exists as a character roaming the Earth in real time. Spin the globe, zoom in, and watch your Normies go about their day and interact with each other.

---

## What is this?

Every Normie that has ever been minted (and not burned) lives on this map. They walk, fly, teleport, and sleep...each one moving according to their type. The world runs 24/7, synced to real time.

---

## The World

- The globe is a real 3D Earth you can spin and zoom freely
- Normies only roam on land, no ocean walkers
- Zoom in close enough and you'll see individual Normies with their portrait and name
- From far away, every Normie appears as a tiny colored dot

---

## Normie Types & How They Move

Each Normie type has its own personality and way of getting around:

| Type | Color | How they travel |
|------|-------|-----------------|
| **Human** | Blue | Flies to new destinations every minute |
| **Alien** | Purple | Teleports anywhere on Earth every 5 minutes |
| **Cat** | Orange | Wanders and follows Humans around |
| **Agent** | Dark | Uses underground tunnels between cities, then flies |
| **THE100** | Gold | Special golden border, always stands out |

---

## Day & Night

The world follows real time (CET timezone). When it's night where a Normie is standing, they go to sleep:

- Sleeping Normies are greyed out and completely still
- The clock in the bottom-right shows how many Normies are awake vs asleep right now
- As the Earth rotates through day and night, the active population shifts

---

## Exploring

- **Spin the globe** - click and drag to rotate
- **Zoom in** - scroll to get closer to any region
- **Click a Normie** (when zoomed in) - opens their collector card with all traits
- **Follow a Normie** - use the side panel to track a specific Normie by number; the camera will follow them around the world
- **Search** - find any Normie by their ID

---

## The Collector Card

When you select a Normie, a compact card appears in the top-right corner showing their portrait, type, and current status. Click the card to expand the full detail view:
- Full portrait
- All traits
- Current holder wallet address
- Link to their OpenSea page
- Current status (awake, asleep, flying, teleporting...)

Press **ESC** at any time to deselect a Normie and return to free exploration.

---

## Conversations

When Normies end up near each other, they talk. Speech bubbles appear above them with type-appropriate dialogue. Humans and Agents speak English, Aliens speak French, Cats meow. Already a classic.
